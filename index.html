Welcome to the Centre for Laboratory Services
Faculty of Science
University of Ruhuna

CLS is a laboratory service facility offering analytical services to researchers in academia, industry, and both public and private sectors. All services are supervised by academic staff from the Faculty of Science.

Our services include:

  • Water quality analysis
  • Standard soil chemical analysis
  • Standard microbiological tests for soil and water
  • PCR for bacteria and fungi identification
  • Cyanotoxin detection and quantification
  • Aflatoxin detection and quantification

Additional offerings:

  • Pure cultures of bacteria and cyanobacteria
  • Pure cultures of fungi

A service charge applies to all users. Concession rates are available for undergraduate (FT-IR analysis only) and postgraduate students enrolled at the University of Ruhuna (UoR).
